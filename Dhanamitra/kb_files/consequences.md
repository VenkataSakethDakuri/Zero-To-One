# Consequences of Non-Payment

**1. CIBIL & Credit Score Impact**
Your default is automatically reported to all four credit bureaus: CIBIL, Equifax, Experian, and CRIF High Mark. A drop in your credit score will disqualify you from future personal loans, home loans, and credit cards with any bank in India.

**2. Financial Penalties**
* **Bounce Charges:** A fee of **Rs. 590** (including GST) is levied for every failed NACH/Auto-debit attempt.
* **Late Interest:** A penal interest rate of **0.2% per day** is charged on the overdue outstanding balance.

**3. Legal Action**
For loan amounts exceeding Rs. 25,000, Dhanamitra Finserv Ltd. reserves the right to initiate legal proceedings under:
* **Section 138** of the Negotiable Instruments Act (Cheque Bounce).
* **Section 25** of the Payment and Settlement Systems Act.
