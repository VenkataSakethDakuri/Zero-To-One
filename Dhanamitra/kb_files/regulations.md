# Regulatory, Privacy & Grievance Redressal

**1. Authorization**
Dhanamitra Finserv Ltd. is a fully compliant Non-Banking Financial Company (NBFC) registered with the Reserve Bank of India (RBI). Registration Number: `B-09.1234`. We are legally authorized to collect dues based on the loan agreement signed by you via OTP.

**2. Calling Hours & Conduct**
* **Hours:** In strict adherence to RBI guidelines, we only contact customers between **08:00 AM and 07:00 PM**. We do not make calls on National Holidays.
* **Conduct:** Our agents are trained to be professional. Harassment is strictly against company policy.

**3. Grievance Redressal**
If you have a complaint regarding agent behavior, please note the date and time of the call and report it to our Grievance Officer at `grievance@dhanamitra.com`.

**4. Data Privacy**
Your personal and financial data is stored securely on servers located within India. We do not sell your data to third parties. Data is shared only with Credit Information Companies (like CIBIL) as mandated by law.
