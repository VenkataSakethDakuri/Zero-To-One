# Extension & Hardship Policy

**1. Standard Extension (Up to 3 Days)**
* **Eligibility:** Available to all customers with a good repayment history.
* **Terms:** We can hold calls and penalties for up to 3 days from the due date if you have a valid reason (e.g., salary delay, medical emergency).

**2. Paid Extension (Up to 7 Days)**
* **Requirement:** Requires Manager approval.
* **Terms:** You must pay a "Commitment Amount" of **10% of the total outstanding balance** immediately to lock in the new date.

**3. Hardship Program (EMI Holiday)**
* **Eligibility:** For severe cases such as job loss or hospitalization.
* **Process:** Please email proof (Termination Letter or Hospital Bill) to `help@dhanamitra.com`. Our risk team will review your case for a possible restructuring or short-term EMI holiday.
